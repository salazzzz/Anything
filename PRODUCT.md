# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Newton, MA car owners who care about how their vehicle looks and want the convenience of mobile detailing. They're busy, have disposable income, and value quality over price. They're not shopping for the cheapest option — they're looking for someone they can trust to do it right without them having to be there.

## Product Purpose

Euro Detailing is a mobile car detailing business run by Eric Salas out of Newton, MA. The site's job is to convert a visitor into a booking — by making the quality of work feel self-evident, and the process feel easy and trustworthy.

## Brand Personality

Premium, precise, quiet. Lets the work speak. No hype, no exclamation marks, no urgency tactics. Confident enough not to shout.

## Anti-references

- Cheap local business sites: WordPress templates, crowded layouts, too many fonts, stock photos of mechanics
- Generic auto shop aesthetics: red/black color schemes, clipart logos, cluttered service menus
- Anything that looks like it was built on a template anyone could download for free

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint over decoration** — every element earns its place. If removing it doesn't hurt the page, remove it.
2. **Work speaks first** — before/after imagery and real outcomes do more than copy ever could. Lead with evidence.
3. **Local confidence** — this is Newton's best detail shop, not a global SaaS. Own that specificity. Don't genericize.
4. **Precision in every detail** — spacing, type scale, and color choices are deliberate. Sloppiness is off-brand.
5. **Quiet authority** — no urgency timers, no sale banners. Trust is built through calm, not pressure.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Basic — good contrast and keyboard navigation. No specialized requirements.
