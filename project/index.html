FAQ

Mobile detailing questions, answered

These are the questions most drivers ask before booking a detail in Newton or a nearby area.

What do I need to provide?

Just a spot for your vehicle and access to a water source and outdoor outlet if possible. If your location doesn’t have either, let us know in advance — we can usually work around it.

How long does a detail take?

A basic interior or exterior usually takes 1–2 hours. Premium tiers run 3–4 hours, and a full premium bundle can take most of the day. Each service page lists a clear time estimate.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the package and the size of your vehicle. Each service page has clear starting prices for basic and premium tiers, plus add-ons if you need anything extra.

Do I need to be home during the detail?

Not at all. As long as we have access to the vehicle and the keys, you’re free to go about your day. We’ll send updates and let you know once it’s finished.

What payment do you accept?

Cash, Venmo, Zelle, and major credit cards. Payment is collected after the service so you can see the finished result first.

Which package should I choose?

If your car is already maintained regularly, a Basic tier is plenty. If it’s been a while or you’re prepping for sale, a Premium tier delivers a much deeper reset.

Do you serve only Newton?

Newton is the primary area, but we also serve nearby communities when the location and job are a good fit. Reach out if you are unsure whether you are inside the service range.

How do I book a maintenance detail?

Maintenance is shown alongside premium service options. Select premium first or click the maintenance option inside the service card to view the matching price and booking link.
