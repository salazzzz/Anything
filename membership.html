Questions

The stuff people ask first.

Two visits a month — isn't that a lot?

It's the opposite. At every two weeks your car never actually gets dirty, so each visit is quick and easy and your car always looks freshly detailed. Fewer visits would mean more grime, harder work, and a car that only looks great part of the time. This is what keeps it perfect.

What if my car needs a full detail first?

That's exactly what your first visit is for. We get the car all the way back to pristine, then the membership keeps it there. If it needs a heavier one-time restoration to start, we'll tell you up front.

How do I pay?

One flat charge a month, collected at your first visit of the month. No card on file to start, no auto-renew traps — you can cancel any time before the next month.

What if it snows or it's freezing?

Your interior gets done every visit no matter what. When it's too cold or actively storming for a proper exterior wash, we roll it to your next visit so your paint is never touched in bad conditions.
